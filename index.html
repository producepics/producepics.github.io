What is PACA?

The Perishable Agricultural Commoditities Act (PACA) was enacted at the request of the produce industry. PACA protects the produce industry by establishing and enforcing a code of fair business practices and helping companies resolve business disputes.

Will the cost of Quality / USDA Inspections go down?

By eliminating the need for a certified inspector to travel and by including a robust image processing platform, we anticipate the cost of inspections to go down from US $ 250 per inspections to around $ 25 per inspection.

Why use Blockchain?

PACA contracts are paper based "smart contracts". By implementing PACA compliant smart contracts in Blockchain one can introduce smart triggers and automatic payments etc. to improve the overall efficiency and reliability.

Will the wait times for Quality / USDA Inspections go down?

Travel time and the need to drive to multiple facilities contribute to large wait times, often in the range of a few hours to upto 25 hours. Use of the roubst image processing platform reduces wait times for inspections.
